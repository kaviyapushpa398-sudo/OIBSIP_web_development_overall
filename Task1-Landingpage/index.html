Core Features

Everything you need to study smarter

Six powerful features designed to make collaborative learning seamless, engaging, and effective for every student.

🪪

Student Login

Sign in using your college student ID and department. Your identity is verified instantly — no long sign-up forms needed.

📚

Course Selection

Browse and select courses available for your department. The system curates content specific to your academic curriculum.

📊

Learning Level

Identify yourself as a Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced learner. Honest self-assessment makes groups stronger and sessions richer.

👥

Auto Group Formation

Our algorithm automatically creates balanced 3-member groups — one from each skill level — so every session has a natural teacher and eager learners.

💬

Private Group Chat

Each group gets a secure private chat room to discuss topics, share resources, ask questions, and collaborate outside of sessions.

⏱️

30-Min Teaching Sessions

Structured 30-minute sessions keep learning focused. The advanced student teaches, while others ask questions and build understanding in real-time.

Process

Up and running in 6 simple steps

01

Log In With Your ID

Enter your college student ID to access the platform. Secure, fast, and tied to your institution.

02

Select Department & Course

Pick your department and the specific course you want to study. The app filters content just for you.

03

Choose Your Level

Select Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced. Be honest — the mix is what makes the magic happen.

04

Smart Group Is Created

The system pairs you with one Beginner and one Advanced peer (or vice versa) for perfectly balanced learning.

05

Advanced Student Teaches

The advanced member leads the 30-minute session, reinforcing their own knowledge while helping others grow.

06

Quiz & Learning Games

After the session, everyone takes a quick quiz and plays learning games to solidify what was covered.

Interactive Learning

Learning that actually sticks

StudyCircle goes beyond passive reading. Every session ends with engaging activities that test and reinforce understanding — making knowledge permanent.

🎯

Post-Session Quizzes

Timed quizzes after every session to test retention and identify gaps while the topic is still fresh.

🧩

Drag & Drop Games

Interactive matching and ordering games make complex topics fun and memorable for every skill level.

🔄

Peer Knowledge Sharing

Students share notes, diagrams, and summaries inside the group — building a mini knowledge base for the course.

Admin & Progress

Structure that keeps everyone on track

📋

Syllabus Submission

Advanced students submit their teaching syllabus to the admin weekly, ensuring sessions are structured, relevant, and aligned to the course curriculum.

🎯

Weekly Goals

Each group sets clear weekly learning goals approved by the admin. This creates accountability and keeps every session purposeful and outcome-driven.

📝

Monthly Tests

Formal monthly assessments are conducted by the admin to measure actual progress, validate learning outcomes, and reward top performers across the platform.
